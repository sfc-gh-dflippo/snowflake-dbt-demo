## Sample data sets

Snowflake provides sample data sets, such as the industry-standard TPC-DS and TPC-H benchmarks, for evaluating and testing a broad range of Snowflake’s SQL support.

[See all](sample-data.md)

[SNOWFLAKE\_SAMPLE\_DATA

The sample database, SNOWFLAKE\_SAMPLE\_DATA, is identical to the databases that you create in your account, except that it is read-only.](sample-data-using.md)

[TPC-DS

TPC-DS models the decision support functions of a retail product supplier. The supporting schema contains vital business information, such as customer, order, and product data.](sample-data-tpcds.md)

[TPC-H

TPC-H is a decision support benchmark. It consists of a suite of business-oriented ad hoc queries and concurrent data modifications.](sample-data-tpch.md)
